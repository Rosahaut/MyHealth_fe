MyHealth – Your Personal Health diary

MyHealth is a user-friendly and helps you to monitor and maintain your well-being. It allows you to easily track key aspects of your health, including exercise, heart rate variability (HRV), and medication.

Measurements

Record your vital signs measurements.

Medications

Keep track of your medications, dosages, and when you have taken it.

With MyHealth, you can take a proactive approach to your health by making informed choices and building better habits for a healthier, happier life!
